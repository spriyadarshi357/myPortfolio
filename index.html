Projects

Smart Dustbin

The smart dustbin is an automatic lid opener system for garbage.

Components Used :- Arduino UNO, Servo motor, Jumper wires, Breadboard, Ultrasonic Sensor, Battery, Dustbin, Cardboard, Ice- cream Stick, Adhesive, Scissor, Cello tape

We are using an ultrasonic sensor so when the person detects in front of the dustbin it sends some signal to the Arduino. Now, according to the distance, we make some conditions like if the distance between the person and the dustbin is less than 30 centimeters then the Arduino will send a command to the servo motor to rotate 90 degrees from its initial position which causes the lid to open and after the interval of 10 seconds, the lid well automatically close.
